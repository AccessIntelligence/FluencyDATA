What is FluencyDATA?

FluencyDATA is your enterprise gateway to intelligent data augmentation. Powered by Active Graph Networks and ActiveShell, we don't replace your existing systems—we amplify them.

Who It's For

  • Enterprise Teams: Bring your SQL/CSV/Blob/NoSQL. We'll bring context and clarity.
  • Architects & Devs: Explore graphs natively in PowerShell. AGI-ready architecture from the shell.
  • Strategic Leaders: See your data landscape in full fidelity. Enforce RBAC, ACL, and audit policy natively.

How It Works

  1. Connect: Hook into your existing data stores.
  2. Generate Graph: Transform and enrich your data with relationships, policies, and inheritance.
  3. Explore: Query through the shell, web, or visual tools like Mermaid & GraphViz.
  4. Augment: Infer, validate, and visualize layers of meaning and compliance.
